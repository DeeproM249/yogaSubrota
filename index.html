Meet Our Founder

Subrota Mahato

Founder & Visionary, YouthLife Yoga Institute

With a deep-rooted belief that yoga is the ultimate path to longevity, Subrota Mahato founded YouthLife Yoga Institute to bridge the gap between ancient yogic wisdom and modern lifestyle needs. His vision is simple yet powerful — make authentic, result-oriented yoga accessible to every individual, regardless of age or fitness level.

Under his leadership, the institute has grown from a small local class to a recognized name in yoga education, producing state and national-level yoga champions. His approach combines scientific understanding of the human body with traditional yogic principles.
